<p><p>The oven bag market can be categorized into two main types: polyester and nylon. Polyester oven bags are known for their high heat resistance and durability, making them suitable for roasting meats and baking. They provide excellent visibility due to their transparent nature, allowing users to monitor cooking progress. On the other hand, nylon oven bags offer flexibility and are often more affordable, but they have a lower heat resistance compared to polyester. Both types cater to diverse cooking needs and preferences.</p></p>
