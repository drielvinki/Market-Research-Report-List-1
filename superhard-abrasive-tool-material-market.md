<p><p>The superhard abrasive tool material market consists primarily of two types: Polycrystalline Diamond (PCD) and Cubic Boron Nitride (CBN). PCD is renowned for its exceptional hardness and thermal stability, making it ideal for machining non-ferrous materials, composites, and ceramics. In contrast, CBN is preferred for hard ferrous materials due to its superior wear resistance and thermal endurance. Both materials are essential in various industrial applications, enhancing machining efficiency and precision in metalworking and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
