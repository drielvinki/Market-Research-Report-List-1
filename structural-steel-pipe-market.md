<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Structural Steel Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spiral Weld Pipe</li><li>Double Submerged Arc Weld (DSAW)</li><li>Electric Resistance Weld (ERW)</li><li>Seamless (SMLS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The structural steel pipe market comprises various types based on manufacturing processes. Spiral Weld Pipe features a spiral seam formed by welding a continuous strip, ideal for larger diameters. Double Submerged Arc Weld (DSAW) involves welding on both sides simultaneously, ensuring high strength and quality. Electric Resistance Weld (ERW) uses electrical resistance to fuse edges, suitable for smaller diameters and thinner walls. Seamless (SMLS) pipes are created from solid steel billets, offering superior strength and reliability without welded seams, making them ideal for high-pressure applications.</p></p>
