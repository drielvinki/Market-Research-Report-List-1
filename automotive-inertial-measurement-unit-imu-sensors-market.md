<p><strong>The Automotive Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs</li><li>FOG-based IMUs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) sensors market comprises primarily two types: MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs and FOG-based IMUs. </p><p>MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs utilize micro-electromechanical systems technology, offering compactness, low cost, and sufficient performance for standard navigation applications in vehicles. </p><p>Conversely, FOG-based IMUs employ fiber optic technology, providing high precision and stability, making them suitable for advanced applications in autonomous vehicles and critical navigation systems. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety and performance through accurate motion sensing.</p></p>
