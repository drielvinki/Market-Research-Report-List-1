<p><p>A food freeze dryer, or lyophilizer, is a specialized equipment used to preserve food by removing moisture. The process involves freezing the food to a very low temperature, followed by the sublimation of ice, effectively drying the product while retaining its structure, flavor, and nutrients. This method is favored for its ability to extend shelf life without the use of preservatives.</p><p>The Food Freeze Dryer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period.
