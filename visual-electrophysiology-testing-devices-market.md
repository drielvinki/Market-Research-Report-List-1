<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Visual Electrophysiology Testing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multifocal Electroretinogram</li><li>Visual-Evoked Responses</li><li>Electroretinogram</li><li>Electro-Oculogram</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Visual electrophysiology testing devices encompass various technologies for assessing visual function and diagnosing ocular disorders. The multifocal electroretinogram evaluates retinal responses at multiple locations simultaneously, while visual-evoked responses measure brain activity in reaction to visual stimuli. The electroretinogram assesses overall retinal health by recording electrical responses to light, and the electro-oculogram monitors eye movements and detects changes in retinal pigment epithelium function. Together, these tests provide comprehensive insights into visual pathways and retinal integrity.</p></p>
