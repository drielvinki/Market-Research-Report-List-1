<p><p>The Electric Vertical Take-Off and Landing (eVTOL) aircraft market is categorized into three main types: Tilt-X, Lift+Cruise, and Multi-copters. Tilt-X aircraft utilize rotating wings or blades that tilt for vertical lift and forward flight, offering high efficiency and speed. Lift+Cruise models feature dedicated vertical lift rotors and separate forward propulsion, enhancing range and payload capabilities. Multi-copters, with multiple rotors, provide stability and versatility, making them suitable for urban air mobility and cargo transport applications. Each type addresses specific needs in the evolving aerospace landscape.</p></p>
