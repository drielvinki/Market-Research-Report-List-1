<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P-PRP</li><li>L-PRP</li><li>L-PRF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy utilizes components of a patient's own blood to promote healing and tissue regeneration. The market is categorized into P-PRP, L-PRP, and L-PRF. P-PRP, which has a higher platelet concentration, is primarily used in orthopedic and aesthetic treatments. L-PRP contains a lower platelet count, favoring applications in chronic wounds. L-PRF, a fibrin-rich matrix, supports slower, sustained release of growth factors, making it ideal for surgical applications and dental procedures. Each type serves distinct therapeutic needs.</p></p>
