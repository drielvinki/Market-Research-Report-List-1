<p><ul><li>Hot Forging</li><li>Cold Forging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal forging machine market can be categorized into hot forging and cold forging. Hot forging involves shaping metals at elevated temperatures, enhancing malleability and reducing fracture risk, making it ideal for complex shapes and high-strength components. In contrast, cold forging occurs at room temperature, which increases material strength through strain hardening, resulting in precise dimensions with minimal waste. Both processes cater to diverse industries, including automotive and aerospace, by providing tailored solutions based on specific material properties and application requirements.</p></p>
