<p><p>The surgical lights market primarily consists of two types: LED and halogen lights. LED surgical lights are favored for their energy efficiency, longevity, and superior brightness, providing excellent illumination while minimizing heat generation. They offer adjustable settings and enhanced color rendering, crucial for precision during procedures. In contrast, halogen lights are traditional options, delivering bright light and often lower upfront costs, but they generate more heat and have shorter lifespans compared to LEDs. Each type serves specific operational needs in surgical environments.</p></p>
