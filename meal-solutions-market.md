<p><p>The Meal Solutions Market is divided into three primary types: Ready to Eat (RTE), Ready to Heat (RTH), and Ready to Cook (RTC). RTE meals are fully prepared and can be consumed immediately, offering convenience. RTH meals require minimal heating before consumption, often found in refrigerated or frozen formats. RTC meals, on the other hand, come with raw ingredients and require cooking but are designed for quick preparation, appealing to consumers seeking the freshness of home-cooked meals without extensive effort.</p></p>
