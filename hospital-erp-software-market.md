<p><p>Hospital ERP software is categorized into two main types: On-Premise ERP and Cloud-Based ERP. On-Premise ERP is installed locally on hospital servers and offers greater control and customization, but requires significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based ERP operates on remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT costs, with easy accessibility for users. While On-Premise suits larger establishments needing tailored solutions, Cloud-Based options appeal to smaller hospitals seeking cost-effective, efficient management without extensive infrastructure.</p></p>
