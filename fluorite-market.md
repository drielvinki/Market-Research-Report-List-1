<p><p>The fluorite market is categorized into several types based on purity and application. Acid grade fluorite, with high purity (typically over 97%), is primarily used in the production of hydrofluoric acid and serves the chemical industry. Metallurgical grade fluorite, with lower purity, is mainly employed in the steel and aluminum industries as a flux to lower melting temperatures and improve metal quality. Other types include ceramic grade and optical grade fluorite, utilized in specific industrial and technological applications.</p></p>
