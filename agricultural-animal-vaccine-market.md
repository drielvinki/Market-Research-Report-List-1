<p><p>The agricultural animal vaccine market primarily includes live attenuated vaccines, inactivated vaccines, and other types. Live attenuated vaccines utilize weakened forms of pathogens to stimulate immunity without causing disease, offering strong and long-lasting protection. Inactivated vaccines, on the other hand, contain killed pathogens, providing a safer option with a potentially shorter immunity duration. Other vaccine types may include subunit, recombinant, or DNA vaccines, which leverage modern biotechnology to elicit immune responses while minimizing adverse effects.</p></p>
