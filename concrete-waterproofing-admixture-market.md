<p><ul><li>Crystalline Waterproofing Admixture</li><li>Pore-blocking Waterproofing Admixture</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete waterproofing admixture market comprises several types, each serving distinct functions. Crystalline waterproofing admixtures form insoluble crystals in the concrete, effectively sealing voids and preventing water penetration. Pore-blocking waterproofing admixtures create a barrier by filling pores within the concrete matrix, enhancing its resistance to moisture. Additionally, other types may include various chemical formulations and polymer-based solutions, tailored for specific applications or performance requirements, contributing to the overall durability and longevity of concrete structures exposed to water.</p></p>
