<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive keyless fob market comprises two main types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows users to unlock and start their vehicles without physically interacting with the key fob, using proximity sensors to detect the fob's presence. Conversely, RKES requires users to press a button on the fob to unlock or start the vehicle. Both systems enhance convenience and security for drivers, catering to evolving consumer preferences in vehicle access and technology.</p></p>
