<p><p>The parenteral feeding device market encompasses various types of infusion pumps, primarily stationary and ambulatory infusion pumps. Stationary infusion pumps are typically used in clinical settings, offering precision and stability for administering nutrients directly into the bloodstream over extended periods. In contrast, ambulatory infusion pumps are designed for portability, allowing patients to receive parenteral nutrition while maintaining mobility. This flexibility enhances patient quality of life, expanding treatment options outside traditional healthcare environments. Both types are essential for managing nutritional needs effectively.</p></p>
