<p><p>The specialty tapes market is divided into two main types: woven and non-woven tapes. Woven tapes are constructed from interlaced fibers, offering durability and strength, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications like packaging and reinforcement. Non-woven tapes, made from bonded fibers, provide versatility and greater adhesive performance for purposes such as insulation and medical applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and healthcare, fulfilling specific needs based on their unique material properties and functionalities.</p></p>
