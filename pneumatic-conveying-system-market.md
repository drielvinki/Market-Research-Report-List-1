<p><strong>The Pneumatic Conveying System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dilute-phase conveying</li><li>Dense-phase conveying</li><li>Air-activated gravity conveying</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic conveying systems can be categorized into several types based on their operation. Dilute-phase conveying uses high-velocity air to transport materials in a suspended state, ideal for fine powders. Dense-phase conveying operates at lower velocities, moving bulk materials more efficiently and with less degradation. Air-activated gravity conveying combines air pressure with gravity to transport heavier materials down a slope. Other types may include specialized systems designed for unique material handling requirements, enhancing overall process efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
