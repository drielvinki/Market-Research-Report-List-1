<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Biosensors for Point-of-Care Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochemical Biosensors</li><li>Thermal Biosensors</li><li>Piezoelectric Biosensors</li><li>Accelerometer Biosensors</li><li>Optical Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biosensors for point-of-care diagnostics encompass various types, each utilizing distinct mechanisms. Electrochemical biosensors detect changes in electrical properties due to biochemical reactions, offering sensitivity in monitoring analytes. Thermal biosensors gauge temperature variations from biochemical interactions, providing rapid results. Piezoelectric biosensors measure mass changes on a surface, enabling precise quantification. Accelerometer biosensors assess movement or changes in gravitational forces, useful in monitoring bodily functions. Optical biosensors rely on light interaction with biological elements for detection, offering real-time and non-invasive testing capabilities.</p></p>
