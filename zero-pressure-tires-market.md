<p><p>The Zero Pressure Tires market features two main types: Self-Supporting and Support Ring. Self-Supporting Tires utilize reinforced sidewalls that can withstand load without air pressure, maintaining structural integrity. In contrast, Support Ring Tires incorporate an additional ring to provide support when the tire loses air, ensuring stability and maneuverability. Both types enhance safety and performance in various driving conditions, appealing to consumers seeking reliable options for vehicles, particularly in scenarios where tire failures could pose significant risks.</p></p>
