<p><p>IT Service Management (ITSM) involves practices aimed at designing, delivering, managing, and improving IT services within an organization. The ITSM market can be divided into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based ITSM offers scalability, accessibility, and lower upfront costs, as services are hosted remotely and accessed via the internet. Conversely, on-premises ITSM requires organizations to install and maintain software locally, providing more control and customization but often at higher initial investments and maintenance efforts.</p></p>
