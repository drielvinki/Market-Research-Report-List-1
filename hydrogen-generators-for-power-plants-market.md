<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrogen generators for power plants primarily include traditional alkaline electrolysers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolysers. Traditional alkaline electrolysers use an alkaline electrolyte solution, typically potassium hydroxide, to facilitate the electrolysis process. They are known for their robustness and lower cost. In contrast, PEM electrolysers utilize a solid polymer membrane as the electrolyte, allowing for faster response times and higher purity hydrogen production. Both technologies play crucial roles in sustainable hydrogen generation to support clean energy initiatives.</p></p>
