<p><p>The 6DoF (Degrees of Freedom) tracking system market can be categorized into two main types: inside-out and outside-in. Inside-out tracking utilizes onboard sensors to determine the device's position relative to its environment, making it suitable for mobile applications. Conversely, outside-in tracking relies on external sensors to monitor the tracked object's position, often providing higher precision in controlled settings like VR installations. Both types cater to different needs in industries such as gaming, virtual reality, and robotics.</p></p>
