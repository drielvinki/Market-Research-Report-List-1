<p><p>EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) building profiles are categorized into two main market types: sulfonation and thermoplasticity. The sulfonation market involves the cross-linking of EPDM to enhance durability and resistance to environmental factors, making it ideal for roofing and seals. In contrast, the thermoplasticity market focuses on the flexibility of EPDM materials, allowing for easy processing and shaping, which is beneficial for applications requiring adaptability. Both types cater to various construction and manufacturing needs, emphasizing performance and longevity.</p></p>
