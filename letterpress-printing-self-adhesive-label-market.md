<p><p>The letterpress printing self-adhesive label market can be categorized into release liner labels and liner-less labels. Release liner labels utilize a backing material that protects the adhesive until application, allowing for easier handling and storage. In contrast, liner-less labels eliminate this backing, making them more eco-friendly and reducing waste. Both types offer unique advantages in applications ranging from product labeling to promotional materials, and their adoption is driven by advancements in printing technology and evolving market demands.</p></p>
