<p><p>The automotive PTC heater market comprises two primary types: PTC water heaters and PTC air heaters. PTC water heaters utilize Positive Temperature Coefficient technology to efficiently heat engine fluids, enhancing performance and reducing warm-up times. Conversely, PTC air heaters focus on heating the vehicle's cabin rapidly by warm air circulation, providing comfort during cold weather. Both types leverage advanced materials for effective heat generation, making them increasingly popular in modern vehicles for improved energy efficiency and emissions reduction.</p></p>
