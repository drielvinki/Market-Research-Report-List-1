<p><p>The Water-based Pharmaceutical Ink market is categorized into three types: solids, semi-solids, and liquids. Solid inks are composed of finely ground pigments and binders, offering durability and opacity for various applications. Semi-solid inks provide a thicker consistency, allowing for enhanced control during printing processes and are often used in specific drug delivery systems. Liquid inks, being fluid, enable smooth application and versatility, suitable for high-speed printing on packaging and labels in the pharmaceutical sector, ensuring effective branding and medication identification.</p></p>
