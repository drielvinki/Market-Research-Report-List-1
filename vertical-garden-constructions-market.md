<p><ul><li>Loose Media</li><li>Mat Media</li><li>Sheet Media</li><li>Structural Media</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vertical garden constructions market includes four primary types of growing media. Loose media consists of soil or similar materials that allow plant roots to spread freely. Mat media uses a dense, felt-like substrate that retains moisture and supports plants. Sheet media features flat, pre-planted panels made from various materials, offering a lightweight option. Structural media, often in modular systems, incorporates engineered materials that provide support, drainage, and aeration for robust vertical gardens, catering to diverse urban gardening needs.</p></p>
