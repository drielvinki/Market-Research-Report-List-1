<p><p>The automobile exterior rearview mirror market is divided into two main types: plane mirrors and convex mirrors. Plane mirrors provide a true representation of the area behind the vehicle, offering a clear view but with a limited field of vision. In contrast, convex mirrors are curved, which broadens the field of view and reduces blind spots, making them ideal for safety. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to different consumer preferences and regulatory requirements in vehicle design.</p></p>
