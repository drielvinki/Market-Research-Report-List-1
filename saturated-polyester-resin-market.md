<p><ul><li>Hybrid Saturated Polyester Resins</li><li>Pure Saturated Polyester Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Saturated Polyester Resin market is primarily categorized into two types: Hybrid Saturated Polyester Resins and Pure Saturated Polyester Resins. Hybrid Saturated Polyester Resins combine polyester with other resins, offering enhanced properties such as improved flexibility and better adhesion, making them suitable for diverse applications. In contrast, Pure Saturated Polyester Resins are formulated using only polyester compounds, providing excellent stability, durability, and chemical resistance, often used in coating and industrial applications. Both types cater to varying performance requirements in the market.</p></p>
