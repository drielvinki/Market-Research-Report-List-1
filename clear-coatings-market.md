<p><strong>The Clear Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Water-borne</li><li>Powder Coating</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clear coatings market is segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **Solvent-borne** coatings utilize organic solvents for application, offering durable finishes but with higher VOC emissions.</p><p>2. **Water-borne** coatings use water as the primary solvent, leading to lower VOC levels and easier cleanup, making them more environmentally friendly.</p><p>3. **Powder coatings** are applied as dry powder and cured under heat, resulting in a tough finish without solvents, ideal for various substrates.</p><p>4. **Other** coatings include specialty formulations that cater to specific applications or requirements, such as UV-resistant or antimicrobial properties.</p></p>
