<p><p>The baby bottle sterilization instrument market includes two primary types: electric steam sterilization and microwave steam sterilization. Electric steam sterilizers use hot steam generated from boiling water to eliminate harmful bacteria and germs, typically accommodating multiple bottles simultaneously. In contrast, microwave steam sterilizers utilize the heat generated inside a microwave to produce steam, offering a quicker and more compact sterilization solution. Both methods ensure safe feeding conditions for infants by effectively sterilizing bottles and accessories.</p></p>
