<p><p>The Digital Breast Tomosynthesis (DBT) system market primarily features two types of technologies: 2D Digital Mammography and 3D Digital Mammography. </p><p>2D Digital Mammography utilizes flat images to detect abnormalities in breast tissue, usually producing standard mammograms. In contrast, 3D Digital Mammography, or DBT, creates a layered image of the breast, allowing for enhanced visualization and improved detection of tumors. This advanced technique reduces overlapping tissue issues, making it a preferred choice for more accurate breast cancer screening and diagnosis.</p></p>
